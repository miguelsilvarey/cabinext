Beyond Cabinets — How CABINEXT Turns Goals Into Kitchens That Work

By Maelo Méndez · Kitchen & Bath Remodeling Specialist, CABINEXT — Orlando, FL · January 2025

At CABINEXT, we know a remodel isn’t just about materials — it’s about meeting the real goals behind your kitchen: comfort, beauty, and value.

96% ROI on minor remodels NKBA: custom cabinetry #1 splurge Designed for family comfort

When people think about remodeling, the conversation often starts with things: cabinets, countertops, tile samples. But at CABINEXT, we’ve learned that homeowners aren’t really chasing things. They’re chasing goals.

For some, it’s a kitchen where mornings aren’t chaos. For others, it’s hosting friends without bumping elbows. And for many, it’s simply making sure the investment adds value to their home.

Experience that speaks louder than promises

Every company can show you glossy photos. What sets CABINEXT apart is the process. Over the years, working across Orlando homes, we’ve refined a way of building that makes homeowners feel guided, not overwhelmed.

  • Recommending moisture-resistant finishes because Florida’s humidity isn’t forgiving.
  • Designing storage layouts that make sense for your daily routine, not just for a catalog photo.
  • Coordinating with reliable suppliers so deadlines are met — because a kitchen under construction for 3 months is not anyone’s dream.

Goals we help you reach

According to the 2024 Cost vs. Value Report, kitchen remodels remain one of the highest-return home projects nationwide, with minor upgrades recouping nearly 96% of costs at resale.

But ROI is just one part of the story. CABINEXT projects aim at three goals:

  1. Functionality — a kitchen where movement feels natural.
  2. Aesthetics — a space that reflects your style, not just trends.
  3. Value — both in daily use and long-term investment.

A story from the field

Last year, we worked with the Rivera family in Lake Nona. Their main goal? “We just want mornings to feel less stressful.”

By adjusting cabinet layout, adding a breakfast island, and layering task lighting, we transformed their kitchen into what they now call “the calmest room in the house.”

The project didn’t just meet the checklist. It met the emotional goal behind it: comfort.

Quick takeaway: At CABINEXT, turning goals into kitchens that work is our everyday experience.
